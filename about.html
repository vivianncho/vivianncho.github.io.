About Me:

As I joined a Science Saturday program hosted by the Lamont Doherty earth observatory because of my interest in aquatic animals, I got interested in helping to promote conservation solutions to help the animals. I began interning at the Green School Alliance program to help create change and connect schools to lead to a more sustainable future. I acquired information about climate change and its effects. Then I soon branched out into learning more about the influence of climate change on wildlife and got the desire to help them. I want to help inform people about the many marine animals that face the heat of climate change.
